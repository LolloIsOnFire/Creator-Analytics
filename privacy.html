1. Data we collect

We collect information you provide directly, data generated by your use of the app, and technical information required to secure and operate the platform.

Examples

  • Account and contact details.
  • Usage data such as TikTok connection events, dashboard interactions, and workflow activity.
  • Device, browser, and log information used for reliability and security.

2. How we use data

We use data to provide analytics features, improve performance, communicate updates, and maintain platform security.

Operational purposes

  • Authenticate users through TikTok OAuth and manage account state.
  • Deliver Dashboard metrics, Insight Engine recommendations, and Content Ideas.
  • Detect abuse, error states, and unusual activity.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with service providers required to run Creator Analytics, or when required by law.

Common recipients

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers including Supabase services.
  • Payment processors.
  • Support and communication tools.

4. Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Deletion requests

Where legally applicable, you may request deletion of certain personal data. Some information may be retained for security, legal, or accounting reasons.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

Creator Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies for session continuity, preferences, and basic analytics.

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect the availability or convenience of certain features.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal data.

How to exercise them

Contact us through the methods listed below and include enough information to help us verify your identity and process your request safely.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how Creator Analytics handles data, contact our team through the channels listed in the footer or through your account administrator.
